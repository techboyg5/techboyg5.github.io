Blog Plan

This is techboyg5's blog plan.

What I have tried before

About two months ago, I was thinking about creating my own blog. For three days, I had a blog written from scratch, but the downside was that whenever I added a new post to my blog or changed an existing one, I had to change every single page in the blog! As a result, I took down my blog three days after I created it.

As a result, I would prefer WordPress. WordPress is free and open source software. With WordPress, changing a single thing is very easy. It is popular for blogging, and it has a lot of themes and plugins. I have been learning WordPress over the past month. Even though WordPress is free and open source software, you still have to pay for a hosting service.

Pricing

I would recommend Bluehost for my blog hosting. It is popular for WordPress hosting, and has been recommended by WordPress.org since 2005.

Bluehost has three WordPress hosting plans. The Basic plan is perfect, and costs $2.95/month. With it, you get one website, 50 GB of storage and a free domain for 1 year.

I think that having my own blog would be a great idea.
